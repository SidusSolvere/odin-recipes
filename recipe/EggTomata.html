Egg Tamata

Description

This recipe comes directly from the daily life of my best friend, a recipie which they often made everday for breakfast, a fast and hearty version of shakshuka filled with the goodness of tomatoes and protein ritch eggs. Eathing this with bread is a whole meal jam packed with nutrients from the veggies, protein and fat from the eggs and carbs from the bread a truly allrounder to get your day started

Ingredients

Serving 1
  • Eggs 2
  • Tomatoes 3
  • Peppers 2
  • Onion 1
  • Garlic 3 cloves
  • Bread 2 slices
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Black Pepper
  • Coriander

Steps

  1. Get your Cast Iron Skilette on medium flame and add a few cubes of butter with a 1Tbsp of Olive oil and let it heat it
  2. Chop your onions and garlic and put them in the pan
  3. Remove the onions and garlic when translucent
  4. Cut tomatoes in half and place them skin side down on low-mid flame, sprinkle salt on the tomatoes and put the lid on
  5. After a few minutes add back the onions and garlic and chopped peppers and smash them
  6. Add a splash of water and cook the raw flavour out of the tomatoes, the indicator will be the smell
  7. After a few minutes when the gravy is thick make small divets and crack your egg init add salt and crushed black pepper on the top
  8. Cover it with a lid and turn the flame to low for a few minutes to cook the eggs to your liking
  9. Toast the bread to your linking
  10. Serve piping hot with crispy toasted bread and Egg Tomata
