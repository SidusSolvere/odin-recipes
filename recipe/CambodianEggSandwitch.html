Egg Tamata

Description

One imagines that cooking a fluffy eggs for an egg sandwich is quite tiresome and a long process, but with the help of science it can be done in mere minutes. A recipe which usually takes 20 minutes to prepare now can be done in 10, so lets get started

Ingredients

Serving 1
  • Eggs 2
  • Corn Flour
  • Oil/Butter
  • Bread
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Whisk your eggs with 1tbsp oil, 1tsp cornstarch and salt to taste
  2. Put your frying pan on medium high and spray a little oil on it
  3. After the oil heats up pour your egg mixture
  4. Mix Constantly till your egg forms waves and is fluffed up
  5. Use a spatula to give it the shape you want and take it off the heat
  6. Toast the inside of your buns and apply Mayonnaise
  7. Now put together your sandwich
